The award was received at a dinner in Sheraton Hotel, Ikeja, on August 12th, 2022.

This award is a great honor for the company as it is the recognition of hard work and dedication to provide solutions to people who are looking for a healthier lifestyle.

“The recognition is not only a tribute to our team and our members, but also an inspiration to continue delivering a world-class fitness experience that our valued members deserve.

We are committed to providing the best experience in health and wellness through always being at the forefront of innovation and technology. Finally, we look forward to continuing to provide unprecedented value, top notch amenities and service delivery excellence.”

The i-Fitness brand which is the largest and fastest growing fitness chain in West Africa has been developing across Nigeria and by influence, Africa since 2015 with over 16 networks of gyms spread across Lagos and Abuja, Nigeria.

This fitness community currently has more than 20,000 active members at its facilities. The mission is to empower individuals to lead healthier lives by providing them access to the tools they need to achieve their goals.
