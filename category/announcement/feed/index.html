The three new branches; Acme, Jakande and Gwarinpa which were launched in the first and second quarter of 2022 respectively, have been of great use to the community dwellers.

i-Fitness is set to open two more branches at Lekki-Maruwa and Ikorodu. While this fitness community might be well known in the Lekki axis of Lagos, Ikorodu is an entirely new community.

The incessant complaint by the Ikorodu dwellers for an opportunity to access a well-furnished and equipped gym did not fall on deaf ears.

“The expansion into Ikorodu is a way of taking advantage of the community’s long-standing desire for an opportunity to access a well-furnished and equipped gym. With the addition of Lekki-Maruwa, we’re very excited about what’s to come.”

With these new and upcoming branches, the i-Fitness community has grown by two more locations, bringing the number of networks to 18.

i-Fitness is the first and fastest-growing fitness chain in West Africa, that aims to promote health and wellness in Nigeria and Africa at large through a variety of fitness classes and a supportive fitness community.

The company’s goal is to create an environment where people can work out in a safe, clean space with qualified trainers who will guide them through each workout.

This consistent push towards expansion is aimed at promoting the culture of fitness in Nigeria and Africa at large.
