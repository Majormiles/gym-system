With these new and upcoming branches, the Perfect Gym community has grown by two more locations, bringing the number of networks to 18.

Perfect Gym is the first and fastest-growing fitness chain in West Africa, that aims to promote health and wellness in Ghana and Africa at large through a variety of fitness classes and a supportive fitness community.

The company’s goal is to create an environment where people can work out in a safe, clean space with qualified trainers who will guide them through each workout.

This consistent push towards expansion is aimed at promoting the culture of fitness in Ghana and Africa at large.
